Barcode Inventory Solution

A simple, affordable, and scalable way to automate your greenhouse inventory using barcodes.

How It Works

  • Web interface to generate barcodes for plant batches
  • Wireless print requests
  • Barcodes scanned for inventory tracking and yield prediction

Demo: Creating and managing barcodes in the webapp

Demo: Printing barcodes using arduino and thermal printer.

Why Choose This Solution?

Most small to medium greenhouses still use pen and paper or spreadsheets for tracking plant inventory. This system bridges the digital gap by offering:

  • Fast deployment
  • No monthly software fee
  • Modular and offline-friendly hardware
  • Scalable for expansion
